Anxiety Disorder Test

Anxiety symptoms can significantly impact our emotions and daily functioning, even affecting overall health. The GAD-7 Anxiety Self-Assessment Scale is a common tool to help assess anxiety over the past two weeks. The scale includes seven main symptoms of anxiety, such as excessive worry, uncontrollable nervousness, emotional instability, sleep disturbances, and more. Anxiety not only causes psychological strain but can also impact our work efficiency, interpersonal relationships, and physical health. Using the GAD-7 scale can help us identify the severity of anxiety symptoms, enabling us to seek professional help when necessary, improving mental and physical health, and enhancing quality of life.

Please answer the questions based on your experiences over the past two weeks.
